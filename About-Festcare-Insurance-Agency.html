Our Journey Through the Years

From our humble beginnings to becoming one of Kenya's trusted insurance and financial service providers, our journey has been defined by growth, innovation, and unwavering commitment to our clients.

2010

Foundation

Festcare Insurance Agency was founded by Samuel Mwangi with a vision to provide accessible insurance solutions to Kenyans. Starting with just 3 employees in a small office in Nairobi.

2013

Expansion

Opened our second branch in Mombasa and expanded our service offerings to include financial planning and investment advisory services, growing our team to 15 professionals.

2016

Strategic Partnership

Joined the Strathwall Group, allowing us to leverage greater resources and expertise to better serve our growing client base across Kenya. Introduced innovative digital solutions.

2019

Award Recognition

Received the "Excellence in Customer Service" award from the Insurance Regulatory Authority of Kenya. Launched our specialized business insurance division.

2023

Digital Transformation

Launched our comprehensive digital platform, allowing clients to manage their policies, investments, and claims online. Expanded to 5 branches across Kenya with over 50 employees.
