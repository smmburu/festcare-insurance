Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about insurance, investments, and our services.

Festcare Insurance Agency offers a comprehensive range of insurance products including life insurance, health insurance, property insurance, motor insurance, business insurance, and specialized coverage options tailored to individual and corporate needs across Kenya.

Starting your investment journey with Festcare is simple. Schedule a consultation with one of our financial advisors who will assess your financial goals, risk tolerance, and time horizon. Based on this assessment, we'll recommend suitable investment products and help you set up your investment account with as little as KSh 5,000 initial deposit.

To file a claim, contact our claims department at 0720 680272 or visit our office in Karen. You'll need to complete a claim form and provide supporting documentation relevant to your specific claim. Our claims team will guide you through the process and work to ensure prompt and fair settlement of your claim.

Festcare offers personalized retirement planning services. Our advisors will help you determine your retirement goals, assess your current financial situation, and develop a tailored plan that may include pension schemes, investment products, and insurance solutions designed to provide financial security during your retirement years.

Festcare stands out through our holistic approach to financial security. Unlike agencies that focus solely on insurance, we integrate insurance protection with wealth management and investment strategies. As part of the Strathwall Group, we have access to extensive resources and expertise, allowing us to provide comprehensive solutions tailored to the Kenyan market and individual client needs.
