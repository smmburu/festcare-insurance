Eligibility Requirements

Understanding eligibility criteria is essential for selecting the right insurance and financial products. Here's what you need to know.

Life & Health Insurance

  • Age Requirements

    Life insurance: 18-65 years for new policies
    Health insurance: No age limit for most plans

  • Documentation

    Valid ID, proof of residence, and completed application form. Medical examination may be required for higher coverage amounts.

  • Health Considerations

    Pre-existing conditions may affect premiums or require waiting periods. Simplified underwriting available for basic plans.

Property & Financial Services

  • Property Requirements

    Property must be in good condition with proper security measures. Valuation may be required for high-value properties.

  • Vehicle Requirements

    Valid registration, inspection certificate, and driver's license. Vehicle age and condition may affect premium rates.

  • Investment Requirements

    Minimum investment amounts vary by product. KYC documentation required including ID, tax PIN, and source of funds verification.

Not sure if you qualify? Our advisors can help determine your eligibility and find suitable alternatives if needed.
